Risk Management

Multiple Layers of Protection

1. Position Sizing (Kelly Criterion)

f* = (p × b - q) / b Where: f* = Fraction of capital to bet p = Probability of winning b = Odds (profit/loss ratio) We use HALF_KELLY for safety margin

2. Execution Safety

  • Atomic trades (both legs or neither)
  • Circuit breaker after 5 failures
  • Maximum 20 concurrent positions

3. Capital Protection

  • 5% daily loss limit
  • Per-trade position caps
  • Minimum liquidity requirements
