Problem Statement

Cryptocurrency and prediction markets exhibit frequent pricing inefficiencies:

  • Cross-venue price discrepancies (same asset, different prices)
  • Structural mispricings in related prediction markets
  • Temporal inefficiencies in date-variant markets
  • Constraint violations in mutually exclusive/exhaustive outcome sets

Solution

PolyMangoBot automates the detection and execution of arbitrage opportunities through real-time market monitoring, ML-enhanced prediction, and atomic multi-leg trade execution.

Trading Strategies

Cross-Venue Arbitrage 40% Capital

Exploits price differences for the same asset across exchanges.

Example

BTC on Polymarket: $42,000 | BTC on Kraken: $42,150

Action: Buy on Polymarket, sell on Kraken

Profit: 0.36% minus fees

Directional Trading 25% Capital

15-minute timeframe momentum trading using technical indicators (RSI, MACD, volume).

Risk Management: Stop-loss at 2%, take-profit at 4%

Micro-Arbitrage 15% Capital

High-frequency, low-threshold arbitrage for consistent small gains.

Parameters: Minimum spread 0.1%, up to 500+ trades/week

Structural Arbitrage 20% Capital

Novel strategy detecting constraint violations in related prediction markets.

Violation Condition Trade
Exclusive Sum YES > 1.0 Sell all YES
Exhaustive Sum YES < 1.0 Buy all YES
Monotonicity Later deadline cheaper Buy late, sell early
NO Sweep Sum NO < 1.0 Buy all NO

Position Sizing (Kelly Criterion)

f* = (p × b - q) / b

Where:
  f* = Fraction of capital to bet
  p  = Probability of winning
  b  = Odds (profit/loss ratio)
  q  = Probability of losing (1-p)

Modes:
  FULL_KELLY    = f*
  HALF_KELLY    = f* / 2  (recommended)
  QUARTER_KELLY = f* / 4  (conservative)
