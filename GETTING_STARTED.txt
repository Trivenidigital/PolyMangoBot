================================================================================
READING THE OPPORTUNITIES
================================================================================

Example output:
  1. DOGE: 15.56% spread, Liquidity score: 3.1, Fill time: 4000ms

What this means:
  DOGE = Market/symbol
  15.56% = Buy-sell price difference
  Liquidity score: 3.1 = Quality metric (higher = better)
  Fill time: 4000ms = How long to execute

High liquidity score = Good trade (system prioritizes these)
Low liquidity score = Filtered out (low quality)

================================================================================
UNDERSTANDING RISK VALIDATION
================================================================================

Example output:
  Risk Level: SAFE
  Safe to trade: YES
  Estimated profit: $71.65
  Profit after fees/slippage: 14.329%
  Position size $500 is within limits

What this means:
  SAFE = Good to trade
  YES = Profitable after all costs
  $71.65 = Estimated profit amount
  14.329% = Profit as percentage
  Within limits = Position size is appropriate

If it said "REJECT" instead:
  - Not profitable enough
  - Risk is too high
  - Skip this opportunity
  - Wait for next one
